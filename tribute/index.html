MIKE TYSON

The Baddest Man on the Planet

Here's a time line of Mike Tyson:


  • 1966 - Born in Brooklyn, New York
  • 1980 - Becomes the youngest heavyweight champion in boxing history at the age of 20
  • 1986 - Achieves the unified heavyweight championship by defeating Trevor Berbick
  • 1990 - Faces legal troubles and is sentenced to six years in prison for the rape of Desiree Washington
  • 1992 - Released from prison after serving three years
  • 1995 - Makes a successful comeback to boxing, winning the WBC and WBA titles
  • 1996 - Loses to Evander Holyfield in a highly publicized match
  • 1997 - In the infamous "Bite Fight," Tyson bites a portion of Holyfield's ear off during a rematch
  • 2003 - Files for bankruptcy despite earning hundreds of millions during his career
  • 2011 - Launches a one-man show, "Mike Tyson: Undisputed Truth," sharing insights into his life and career
  • 2020 - Announces a comeback to boxing exhibitions at the age of 54

"Mike Tyson, often known for his ferocious fighting style and tumultuous personal life, has left an indelible mark on the world of boxing. His journey from a troubled youth in Brooklyn to becoming the heavyweight champion of the world reflects the highs and lows of a legendary career. Despite controversies and setbacks, Tyson's impact on the sport and popular culture remains a captivating story of resilience and redemption."
